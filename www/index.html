Map help

Moving an asset

Tap and press on an asset while dragging your finger to move the asset to a new position on the map.

Changing asset settings

Double tap, i.e. tap twice quickly on an asset. An "Asset settings" page should open to allow you to change the following:

  • Title
  • Colour
  • Add and remove text notes

Add asset

Choose an image from a list of icons to represent your asset.

Remove asset

Tap on an image until you see a set of squares around the image. Then press the remove asset button and the asset and its notes will be removed from the map.

Add text

Add a text icon to the map.

Notes

Shows a full list of names of assets together with their notes. If your iPad has a valid email account then you'll be able to email this text.

Map options

Edit map details, take a screenshot of the map or change the display and colour of the background image.
